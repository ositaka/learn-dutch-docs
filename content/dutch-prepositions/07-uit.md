### country / city of birth

> "Ik kom **uit** Portugal."
> _— I am from Portugal._

> "Ik kom **uit** Lissabon."
> _— I am from Lisbon._

---

### going outside

> "Wij gaan **uit** eten."
> _— We're going out for dinner._

> "Wij gaan **uit** wandelen."
> \_— We're going out for a walk.

---

### it's an adverb

> "De lamp is **uit**."
> _— The lam is off._
